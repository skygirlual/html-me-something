If this page is about me, then I'll have to stop and ask myself "Who am I?" which is usually followed by "What am I doing?" and hopefully I know the answers to at least one of those questions! ♥

Talk Baseball to me!

Scratch that, let's talk Royals! I grew up in KC and it was a pretty big deal for our family to go to the games! My dad wasn't the kind of guy to get excited about sports, so it made a big impression when he ran outside cheering after the 1985 World Series win! We've cheered on the Royals all these years, win or lose!

Speaking of Family...

Kids are great. I highly recommend having some if you have time and energy! Experiencing life with my child has been my favorite part of this journey so far! I've found that I enjoy my favorite things more when I'm with my little family.

Good times together include:

  • concerts
  • escape rooms
  • traveling
  • photography
  • and even coding!

Take me far away!

... just be sure I get a return ticket. I LOVE to travel. I love to experience new cultures and see who lives on the other side of the world. People are so interesting, and everyone has a story. I think most people are like that, which is probably why "Humans of New York" is so popular.

I've been to some pretty cool places, some I've been lucky enough to have stayed a while like Paris and Johannesburg.
